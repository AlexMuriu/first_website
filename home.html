Who are we?

Hii Ni Afrika is a website that covers educational stuff on the African Continent. Hii Ni Afrika is Swahili for This is Africa. The website seeks to provide a positive outlook of the African continent by sharing content that portrays an unbiased outlook of the continent. Our content spans emerging news on social-economic and political issues on the continent as well as blog posts, videos and pictures on Africa's diverse cultures, history, flora and fauna.

Interesting facts on the continent:

  • Africa is the 2nd largest continent after Asia
  • Africa is home to the longest river in the world - the Nile
  • More than 1500 languages are spoken in the continent
  • The tallest, largest and fastest animals are found in Africa
  • The largest hot desert - The Sahara - is found in Africa
